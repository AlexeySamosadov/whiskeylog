A whiskey collection tracker that knows what your shelf is worth

Once a collection grows past a dozen bottles, two questions get hard to answer from memory: what do I actually own, and what is it worth? WhiskeyLog is a simple collection tracker built around both. Each bottle records its distillery, region or type (Scotch, Bourbon, Rye, Irish, Japanese and more), age, ABV, the price you paid, and a status — sealed, open or finished — so your shelf and your "already drunk" list stay separate.

The collection view sums it all up at a glance: total bottle count, how many are open versus sealed, the total value of what you've spent, and your average rating across everything you've scored. That's genuinely useful for insurance notes, for deciding whether to open a special bottle, or just for the quiet satisfaction of seeing the shelf laid out. Pro adds a deeper value report that breaks the total down by status and by region, plus your top-rated bottles. Everything stays on your device — private, offline, no account — and it's free for as many bottles as you like.
